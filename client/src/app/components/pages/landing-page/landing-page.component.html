Place your bid.

Auctonary is a plattform for everyone to sell their stuff. Simply add an auction and get started.

  • Give it a name: Successfully selling your products will be easier with a meaningful name
  • Describe your product: Whether it's the design, the condition or your personal story, describing your product in the best way will catch people's attention
  • Stop getting lowballed: Set a minimum price for your product. This way nobody place a bid that will hurt your feelings - deeply.
  • How long will it take to sell it? It's up to you! Set the duration of the time the auction will last.
